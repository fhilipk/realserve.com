A service specific to your needs

An accurate building survey and preparation of an As-Built or Existing Conditions floor plan is one of the most important elements of the design stage of a renovation or refurbishment.

A completed building seldom corresponds exactly to the original plans in every detail. This normally occurs because of unforeseeable on-site complications and variations from the original plans. If inaccurate drawings are relied on during the refurbishment, costly variations will certainly follow during construction.

Surveys include:

AS-BUILT/EXISTING CONDITION PLANS

REFLECTED CEILING plans

BUILDING ELEVATIONS AND SECTIONS

SERVICES PLANS

SITE PLAN DRAWINGS
